Banana Vitamin

How to make a nice banana vitamin

In this page you'll learn how to make a nice and smooth banana vitamin.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 orange
  • 1/3 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Water or milk

Recipe

Before we contiunue you gotta prepare the ingrients 1st

How to Freeze Bananas for Smoothies

  1. Rinse and chop the fresh fruit
  2. Pat them dry, the arrange them on a parchment-paper-lined baking sheet
  3. Slide the baking sheet into the freezer and freeze until hard (about 2 hours).
  4. When the fruit is hard, divide it between freezer-safe bags and freeze it for your next smoothie. They will last up to 2 months.

Directions

  1. Roughly chop the banana and orange quarters, then add to a blender.
  2. Add yogurt and water (or milk).
  3. Turn the blender on and blend until creamy and smooth.
  4. Taste, and then adjust with honey if needed.
